**üí≠1. GAME IDEA.üí≠**
---

* Our game idea consists of two levels through which graphically represent the possible problems when creating a password that is easy to access, and we will also represent through another level the continuous fraudulent messages that we receive (phishing).

* Our project will be developed on a computer screen. There will be a main screen where the character interacts with a computer screen, and from there, the main game interfacce will open (play, volume, list of levels).

---

**2. INTERFACES.**
---

* Our project will have a main where we can play the game, control the volume and access the list of levels.

* Once we start the game, we will have one level or another in which we will have to select passwords or emails (good or bad).

* You will have a final screen when you pass the levels.

* We will have a 'life' interface (on the left, at the minimum, we will have the image of a computer in good condition; on the right, a damaged computer and on the top of that we will have a silhoutte of a person (simulating the person behind the attack)) where a bar will fill up when we make a mistake.

---

**üïπÔ∏è3. MECHANICS.üïπÔ∏è**
---

* When running the game, a person will move to a computer and interact with it to enter the game interface.

* Once inside the interface, we can choose from several options, but if we choose the play option, we will be taken to a first level where we will have to choose the correct option or options from a series of words (passwords). This is done with the mouse or the arrow keys.

* Moving on to the second level, we will see a screen where emails arrive and we have to choose wether they are good or bad, moving them ti the left or right using the mouse ir the arrow keys.
